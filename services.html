Commercial Lighting Scheme

Upgrade your commercial property to energy-efficient LED lighting through government-backed rebate programs. Our certified team handles everything from site assessment to installation, helping you cut operating costs and improve your business's sustainability profile with zero hassle.

  • Government rebates covering most upfront costs
  • LED upgrades reduce energy consumption by up to 70%
  • Suitable for offices, warehouses, retail stores & more
  • Professional assessment and seamless installation included

HEER Scheme Lighting (Small Business)

The High Efficiency Equipment Replacement (HEER) Scheme is a NSW Government initiative helping small businesses replace inefficient lighting with modern LED alternatives at minimal upfront cost. Ecolux manages the entire process — from eligibility check to completed installation.

  • Eligible businesses pay little to no upfront cost
  • Replaces fluorescent & halogen fittings with LED
  • Reduces lighting energy use by up to 80%
  • Fast turnaround with minimal business disruption

Hot Water Systems & Air Conditioning

Replace old, inefficient hot water systems and air conditioning units with modern, energy-efficient alternatives through government rebate programs. Our team assesses your current setup and recommends the best solution to significantly reduce your bills and improve comfort.

  • Heat pump hot water systems eligible for rebates
  • Reverse-cycle air conditioning upgrades available
  • Significant reduction in household energy consumption
  • Available for residential and commercial properties

Insulation (Roof & Wall)

Improve your property's thermal efficiency with quality roof and wall insulation. Government rebate programs reduce upfront costs, helping you achieve a more comfortable, energy-efficient home or business all year round — cooler in summer and warmer in winter.

  • Roof and wall insulation options available
  • Improves indoor comfort in all seasons
  • Government rebates available for eligible properties
