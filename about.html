Our Story

Australia's Reliable Residential & Commercial Solar Partner

ECOLUX Solutions was founded with a single purpose: to make clean, affordable solar energy accessible to every Australian home and business. With decades of combined industry expertise, our team of fully licensed and insured technicians delivers seamless, end-to-end solar installation services across NSW, SA, ACT, and VIC.

From small residential rooftops to large commercial facilities, we design and install customised solar systems built to perform reliably for two decades and more. Every project is backed by our commitment to quality, compliance, and genuine long-term partnerships with our clients.

Our Mission

To empower Australians with energy independence through trusted, high-quality solar solutions — reducing power bills, cutting emissions, and building a sustainable future one installation at a time.
